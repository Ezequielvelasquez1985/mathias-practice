Negative & Interrogative

❓ Reglas

❌ NEGATIVE FORM
Agrega not entre have/has y el past participle:
Lisa has not received the letter.
The boys have not practiced all week.
También: hasn't / haven't
❓ INTERROGATIVE FORM
Pon Have/Has al inicio, luego el sujeto + past participle:
Have you done your homework?
Has he thanked them for the gift?
I/you/we/they → Have | he/she/it → Has
